AND -----> &  sets each bit to 1 if both bits are 1

OR -----> |  sets each bit to 1 if at least one is 1

XOR -----> ^ toggling

NOT -----> ~ invert bits

shift operations:
left ----->  << shifts bits to left
right ----->  >> shifts bits to right

set   num |= (1 << n);
clear num &= ~(1 << n);
toggle num ^= (1 << n);
